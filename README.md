# Nightflix

### Nightflix is an iPhone and iPad app to stream movies and series for free, with a smooth interface.

The app uses TMDB to show movie and series information, such as titles, posters, trending content, popular releases, top-rated items, descriptions, and search results. When you add your own streaming provider, Nightflix can open that provider's player for a selected movie or episode, if the provider has it available.

Nightflix is not a streaming service by itself. It does not include movies, series, channels, subscriptions, or hosted video files. It is a viewer and launcher for content information from TMDB and playback through a provider that you add yourself.

## What you can do

- Browse trending movies and series.
- Search for movies and TV shows.
- View posters, years, categories, and basic details.
- Open movies or episodes through your added provider when available.
- Continue watching items you previously opened.
- Save titles to My List.
- Browse categories.
- Change appearance, animations, and haptic feedback.
- Clear watch history, cached TMDB data, or setup details from Settings.

## How it works

Nightflix has two main parts:

1. TMDB is used as the catalogue. This is what lets the app show which movies and series exist, together with their posters and details.
2. Your streaming provider is used for playback. You add the provider URL during setup or later in Settings. When you press play, Nightflix tries to open the matching movie or episode through that provider.

Whether a title can actually play depends on your provider. If the provider does not have that title, does not support the link format, or is unavailable, Nightflix cannot play it.

## First setup

When you open Nightflix for the first time, the app asks for:

1. A TMDB Read Access Token.
2. A movie provider URL.

The TMDB token is required because Nightflix needs it to load the movie and series catalogue. The provider URL is used only for playback. You can skip the provider during setup and add it later from Settings.

## Settings

From Settings, you can edit your TMDB token and provider, change the app appearance, control animations, enable or disable haptic feedback, and delete local data such as history, cache, or saved setup details.

## Important notes

Nightflix does not own, host, upload, or provide any movies or TV shows. Playback depends entirely on the provider added by the user.

Use Nightflix only with streaming providers and content that you are allowed to access. If a provider is offline, unsupported, or does not contain a selected title, playback may fail.

Nightflix uses TMDB for movie and TV metadata but is not endorsed, certified, or otherwise approved by TMDB.
